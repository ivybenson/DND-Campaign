Druid Loxodon

As Mevdved's supporters grew restless with the current situtation, they took to harrassing farmers and rural villages in order to make the people seek out the protection of a war lord. In a skirmish the Mevred's men Osa's father was killed. With her druid magic and strong heart, she has taken to protecting her neighbors and healing those struck by their anger.

Desmond

Captain Krilljoy Gumbo the 42nd

Dwarven Swashbuckling Rogue

Gumbo is a lifelong vagrant who discovered his passion for treasure at an early age, and has been blending into the crowds of Glavni, stealing and thieving, attempting to get enough cash to get his own ship and realize his dream of being a pirate and to seek out the treasure island of local folk-tales. Gumbo had to leave town after coming into a particularly good asset after breaking into a high-ranked officials home and is currently headed for Shiphome in hopes to cash out and assemble a crew ready to voyage to the rumored treasure island.
